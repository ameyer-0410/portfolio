[Repository](https://github.com/ameyer-0410/ICU-Occupancy-Change-Prediction-in-California)

### Household Hardships and Mental Health Outcomes in the United States
Examines the relatinship between economic stressors and mental health outcomes across U.S. States using a state-by-month analytic framework. The primary objective is to identify which economic conditions are most closely associated with short-term changes in the prevalence of anxiety and depression. The analysis emphasies food insecurity as a household-level indicator of material hardship and compares its association with mental health outcomes to more traditional macroeconomic measures such as unemployment and inflation.

[Repository](https://github.com/ameyer-0410/Household-Hardships-and-Mental-Health-Outcomes-in-the-United-States)

### Childcare Affordability
Analysis focused on how childcare affordability has changed over a 10-year period using the national Database of Childcare Prices. Analysis includes policy brief and Tableau dashboard (as a snapshot) to highlight the growing financial burden of childcare across American families emphasizing that affordability has worsened despite inflation adjustments.

[Repository](https://github.com/ameyer-0410/Childcare-Affordability)
